Unraid Connect

Unraid Connect is a plugin that lets you access your Unraid server remotely (if port-forwarding supported on router), backup the flash device, remote monitoring of the server, and management of the Unraid license.

Unraid Connect

Before Tailscale and our internet being put behind a CGNAT router by the ISP, remote access was the primary benefit. Now, the primary benefit is having the flash drive automatically backed-up to Unraid's site. However, this is not critical either as the plugin Appdata Backup also backs-up the flash drive.
